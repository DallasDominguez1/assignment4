The Snail Scale Project

Dallas Dominguez Concept Website


Snail Scale is a product for those looking for fast and accurate nutrient information about the foods they eat. People don't want the hassle of downloading a nutrient info tracking app and doing all the tedious arithmetic themselves to calculate their nutrients. We provide all of that information by simply placing simple food on our scale.

In this assignment I got to play around with some potential features that could be on our site. Of the CSS I learned, the top 3 things were the power of flex, how to do hover animations, and how to achieve good padding with rem metrics. Thanks Rick for the Dojo!

This is just a rough design for an embedded keypad for the website where a user could enter specific bodily metrics based on questions we ask them or something of the sort.
